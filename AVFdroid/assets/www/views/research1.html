You can also use twitter to just authenticate a user to your specific application that doesn’t involve twitter.

  2. Visit Programmableweb.com and search for JSON-P data sources. Why would we need JSON-P, specifically?

    3. The specific reason to use JSONP is to overcome the cross-domain data domain security policy named same origin policy. This policy inherently restricts a client from making a call to a resource on a different domain. JSONP takes advantage of the script tag as a way to make calls across domains and provides us with a dataset that we can parse and use.
